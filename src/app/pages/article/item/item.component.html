We love startups

2 25.05.2015

Magic dwells in each beginning.
– Hermann Hesse

Especially when it comes to startups and young entrepreneurs, we couldn’t agree more. It always is a magical moment when ideas are born and, much more than that, when ideas become reality. When we work with startups and young entrepreneurs, we always enjoy collaboratively creating new ideas and making them happen. As representative of Adwyse & Co., our partner Thilo Plikat attended the StartUp Weekend Munich - and his ability to tell great stories prevailed. In cooperation with an intercultural and interdisciplinary team, Thilo pitched the business idea of “Jobninja”, an app that makes matching between employer and employees of mini-jobs easier and more convenient.

The jury consisting of heads of top consulting firms, incubators and founders were convinced, and named team “Jobninja” the most innovative team 2015 of the StartUp Weekend Munich. We congratulate Thilo on his achievement, and look forward to support the Jobninja crew in making their idea become reality.
